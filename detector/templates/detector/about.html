Our Mission

In a digital age where scams are becoming increasingly sophisticated, AI Fraud Shield stands as a beacon of protection. Our mission is to empower every Kenyan with free, accessible, and powerful tools to detect and avoid digital fraud before it happens.

Our Story

2024

The Beginning

AI Fraud Shield was born from a simple observation: Kenyans were losing millions to increasingly sophisticated digital scams, and existing solutions weren't accessible or tailored to local threats.

2025

Growth & Impact

We expanded to detect SMS, email, WhatsApp, and call scams with 200+ Kenyan-specific patterns. Our community grew to thousands of users reporting and blocking scam numbers.

2026

Innovation

Launched Corporate Shield for businesses, ML-powered hybrid detection, USSD service (*483*72#) for feature phones, and automated scam takedown systems.
