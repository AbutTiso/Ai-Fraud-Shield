Detection Tools

We detect 200+ Kenyan scam patterns including:

  • Fake M-Pesa transaction messages
  • Account suspension threats
  • Prize/lottery winning scams
  • Fake loan offers (Fuliza, M-Shwari)
  • Bank impersonation (KCB, Equity, Co-op)
  • KRA tax refund scams
  • Job offer scams

To analyze WhatsApp chats:

  1. Open the WhatsApp chat
  2. Tap ⋮ Menu → More → Export chat
  3. Choose "Without media"
  4. Copy the exported text
  5. Paste into our WhatsApp detection tool

Upload your screenshot to our Screenshot Analysis tool. We use OCR (Optical Character Recognition) to extract text and verify:

  • Transaction amounts and formatting
  • M-Pesa confirmation patterns
  • Suspicious phone numbers
  • Fake receipt indicators

Our URL checker analyzes:

  • Domain reputation - checks for suspicious TLDs (.tk, .ml, .xyz)
  • Brand impersonation - detects fake Safaricom, KCB, etc.
  • URL structure - identifies phishing patterns
  • HTTPS status - checks for secure connections

We NEVER visit the link - all analysis is done safely on the URL text.

Reporting & Community

You can report scam numbers in several ways:

  • Use our Report Scam page
  • Forward SMS scams to 333 (Safaricom) or 3333 (Airtel)
  • Dial *483*72# on any phone (USSD service)
  • Report to DCI Kenya at 0800 722 203

Forward phishing emails directly to Kenya's cybercrime unit at report@kenyacic.go.ke. You can also use our Report Phishing page to help our detection system learn new patterns.
