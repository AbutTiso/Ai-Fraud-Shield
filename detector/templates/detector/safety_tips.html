The Golden Rule of Scam Prevention

If something seems too good to be true, it probably is. Always verify through official channels before taking any action.

  • Never click links in unsolicited SMS messages
  • Never share your PIN or M-Pesa password with anyone
  • Verify urgent requests by calling official numbers directly
  • Report scam SMS to 333 (Safaricom) or 3333 (Airtel)
Common scam: "Your M-Pesa account will be suspended"
  • Never click suspicious links hover first to see real URL destination
  • Check sender's email carefully - scammers use similar addresses
  • Don't open attachments from unknown or unexpected senders
  • Forward phishing emails to report@kenyacic.go.ke
Common scam: "Your email account will be closed"
  • Call to verify urgent money requests from family/friends
  • Never share codes verification codes are for YOU only
  • Block & report suspicious contacts immediately
  • Enable 2-step verification in WhatsApp Settings > Account
Common scam: "Mum/Dad I lost my phone, send money to this number"
  • PIN is PRIVATE never share with anyone - even "Safaricom staff"
  • Use official app verify receipts in M-Pesa app or *334#
  • Know your recipient don't send money to unknown numbers
  • Verify screenshots report fake M-Pesa receipts immediately
Common scam: Fake M-Pesa payment confirmation messages
  • Fake profiles verify accounts before engaging with them
  • Fake giveaways legitimate companies don't ask for fees to claim prizes
  • Job scams real employers don't ask for payment to hire you
  • Privacy settings limit who can see your personal information
Common scam: "Work from home earn Ksh 5,000/day"
  • Take your time scammers create urgency to pressure you
  • Research first search online for similar scams reported
  • Protect financial info never share bank details on calls or messages
