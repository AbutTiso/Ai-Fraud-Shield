Safety Tips

How to protect yourself from digital scams

SMS Safety

  • Never click links in unsolicited SMS
  • Never share your M-Pesa PIN
  • Verify urgent requests by calling official numbers
  • Report scam SMS to 333 (Safaricom) or 3333 (Airtel)

Email Safety

  • Never click links in suspicious emails
  • Check sender's email address carefully
  • Hover over links to see real destination
  • Forward phishing to report@kenyacic.go.ke

WhatsApp Safety

  • Verify urgent money requests with a phone call
  • Don't share verification codes
  • Block and report suspicious contacts
  • Enable two-step verification

M-Pesa Safety

  • Never share your M-Pesa PIN with anyone
  • Verify receipts in your official M-Pesa app
  • Don't send money to unknown numbers
  • Report fake receipts immediately
Golden Rule: If something seems too good to be true, it probably is. Always verify through official channels!
